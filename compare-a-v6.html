Why these metrics are not on one scale
Pool firewall AIVIS keeps two separate prompt pools and never blends them silently. Visibility %, Share of Voice %, Position and Citations are measured against the unbranded pool only (category prompts that never name your brand, so they show whether AI surfaces you unprompted). Net Sentiment (NSI) is measured across both pools combined (every prompt, branded and unbranded), so it reflects how AI frames your brand whether or not the prompt named you directly. We label each metric row with its pool and never average a branded-only figure into an unbranded one.
