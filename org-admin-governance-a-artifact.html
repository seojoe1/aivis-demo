Governance

Compliance surface for Meridian Digital: an append-only audit log of security-relevant events, and a DSAR workflow to export or erase all data in scope, GDPR-style.

Data retention

How long records are kept before automated disposal.

Audit log retention

7 years

Append-only. Never deleted within the window; actor references are anonymised after the deactivated-data window.

Deactivated-org data

90 days

Disposed automatically after an organisation is deactivated. Disposal job runs weekly.

Disposal mode

Dry run

The disposal job logs what it would remove without deleting. Switch to live only after reviewing the dry-run audit entries.
