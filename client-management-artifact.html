Client Management

Where an agency (or any multi-brand account) manages its whole book. The plan sits at agency level, not per client: brand slots, prompt allowance and seats up top, then the per-brand roster with tracking coverage, onboarding and lifecycle below.
What's real vs. invented on this page
Real Brand and workspace identity (orgs, workspaces, brands); the brand limit (org_entitlements.brand_limit, null = unlimited); prompts tracked (portfolio vs tracked prompts); team member count (users - seats are unlimited by design, so no denominator is shown); date added (brands.created_at); brand status.
Invented Prompt allowance (no plan-level prompt quota column exists in the schema today - shown as a proposal) and onboarding status (no schema column - needs a migration).
