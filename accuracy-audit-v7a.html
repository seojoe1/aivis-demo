Accuracy at a glance i Classification buckets Five mutually exclusive buckets, drawn directly from the backend claim status and Tier-1/Tier-2 verification flags. They always sum to the period's total checked claims. Correct, Incorrect and Unverified come from the Tier-2 verdict. Uncertain / Not understood covers both a low-confidence Tier-1 read and a response the classifier could not parse at all. Pending is a claim still waiting on Tier-2 verification.

i Pending Awaiting deeper Tier-2 verification. Tier-1 has already flagged the claim as likely correct or worth checking, but it has not yet cleared the confidence, source-match and source-date gates that produce a final Correct / Incorrect / Unverified verdict. Not the same as Uncertain / Not understood, which covers a low-confidence or unparseable response. Pending claims move to one of the other four buckets once Tier-2 completes; this bucket is expected to shrink towards zero within a period or two.
